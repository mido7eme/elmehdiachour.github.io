Biography

I am a PhD candidate at the Inria Lille’s Scool team (ex SequeL), under the supervision of Odalric Maillard (CNRS / Inria Lille) and Vianney Perchet (ENSAE / Criteo AI Lab). Before my PhD, I graduated from École Polytechnique and ENS Paris-Saclay.

I am interested in research related to multi-armed bandits, online learning and reinforcement learning.
